Impractical Jokers

88/100

Impractical Jokers is a show on TruTV about a group of 4 friends who tell each other what to do in public and usually ends up being very embarrassing for the person out in public. By completing every task asked of them the one in public wins and does not get a thumbs down, the person with the most thumbs down at the end does a challenge usually correlating to their fears.

This show gets an 88/100 because it is very funny but can be repetitive and slightly inappropriate to watch.

Adventure Time

96/100

Adventure Time is about a young adventurous boy named Finn and his magical dog named Jake. The two of them, along with other characters, such as a princess made of gum, a flying rainbow-shaped and colored Korean unicorn, and a tiny green elephant, go on various adventures throughout the world. Their main enemy, the Ice King, tries to steal princesses and Finn and Jake always stop it from happening

This show gets a 96/100 because it has a good story, is funny, and has great characters but it is also meant mostly for kids so there are a lot of dumb jokes throughout the show.

Dandadan

98/100

Dandadan is an anime about Ken Takakura and Momo Ayase who each believe in a different paranormal idea. Ken believes in aliens and Momo believes in ghosts, but it turns out both are true and they have to fight against both in order to save themselves as well as other people around them.

This show gets a 98/100 because it has a good story, is funny, and has great characters but it is also very inappropriate and not meant for a wide range of audiences
