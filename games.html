Minecraft

95/100

Minecraft is a game centered around creativity where the goal as the player is to get to the End Dimension and beat the ruler there known as the Ender Dragon and although beating the Dragon is the goal, the main purpose is to gather resources and create things within the world.

This game gets a 95/100 because it is very fun to play by yourself or with friends and can be played basically infinitely but can get repetitive.

Game Site Wiki

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

100/100

The Witcher 3 is a game centered around Geralt of Rivia as he tries to find Ciri, a girl he trained as a child to fight monsters, as she is getting chased by the Wild Hunt. The wild hunt are beings who desire Ciri's power and are trying to take her for it. There is magic, swords, and monsters all in this medieval warzone atmosphere.

This game gets a 100/100 because it has a very detailed story, amazing mechanics, great characters but the one downside of the game is that when you are done with it any other game feels mediocre.

Game Site Wiki

Portal 2

79/100

Portal 2 is an amazing sequel to the first Portal game and is based around this gun that can shoot two separate portals and you need to use them to solve puzzles and make your way through the world while dodging obstacles.

This game gets a 79/100 because although the story is good and the puzzles are challenging and has cool mechanics the game feels very linear and does not have much "meat" in the plot. This is not to say the game is bad in the slightest.
