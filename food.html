Potstickers

89/100

Potstickers are a type of dumpling consisting of a thin layer of dough wrapped around a filling typically made of meat and vegetables. They are prepared by boiling or steaming to ensure the filling is fully cooked and the wrapper is tender, followed by frying to create a crispy, golden-brown outer layer.

Baked Mostaccioli

85/100

Baked Mostaccioli is a traditional pasta dish prepared by layering Mostaccioli pasta with a rich meat sauce and a blend of cheeses. The assembled dish is baked in the oven until the cheese is fully melted, the flavors are well-integrated, and the top achieves a slightly crisp, golden finish.
